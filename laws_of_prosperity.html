The Laws of Prosperity: 

A New Paradigm to Create the Future

By Ryuho Okawa

 

Preface

Following last year’s Happiness Revolution which became a best-seller, it is my strong, strong desire to turn this writing, The Laws of Prosperity, into a law of salvation or the spirit of salvation, to save humanity from the veil of darkness amid the turn of this century. 

            Furthermore, the economic crisis that is haunting Japan and the rest of the world is not merely an economic phenomenon, but rather a crisis for humanity; it is the pain of creation that comes alongside the building of a fundamental paradigm (a framework for thinking and perception) for the next millennium. 

            While this book includes topics of current affairs, the essence of The Laws of Prosperity is on education, on the spiritual world, on living a successful life, and a guide for companies and even nations to get themselves out of a financial crisis. I have also spoken on a revolution in people’s values surrounding faith. 

            A true prosperity in Japan and the rest of the world from the 21st century onwards will begin from this one book that was published in an Asian country. Will you – and the people of this country – be able to withstand fundamental values being shaken? It may come as a shocking jolt, and the stake is the survival of humanity.

January 1999

Happy Science Founder, Ryuho Okawa
